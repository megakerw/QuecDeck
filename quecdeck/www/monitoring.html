Configuration summary
  • Reboots the modem after failed pings in a row (~ without response).
  • If the link stays down, each reboot needs twice the failed-ping streak of the last, up to a ~2-hour gap between reboots.
  • Reboots are recorded on the Logs page.
  • reboots after only seconds without a response, so a brief network blip can trigger an unnecessary reboot. Raise the interval to at least seconds, or add more failures, to keep a full-minute margin.
